Graphics

Mosaic, at its core, captures the energy of designers, developers, and marketers - each shape displays a vital piece in bringing ideas and solutions together. To represent this, our graphics revolve around bold geometric elements that mirror this energy; uniting them into a cohesive, inspiring whole.

Inspiration

Inspired by minimalist and playful geometry, we drew inspiration from shapes and colours to create a smooth, vibrant look. These references guided the use of our modern colour blocks, geometric icons, and clean, structured layouts—ensuring that the final graphics felt energetic, inviting, and true to the Mosaic identity.

Grid

The grid system for the app is designed with a clean and consistent layout, featuring 4 columns. Each column has a height of 16px, with a 16px offset from the screen edges and 16px gutters between columns. This structure ensures a balanced and visually organized interface, supporting responsive design and easy alignment of UI elements.
