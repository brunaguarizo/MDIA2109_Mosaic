Logo and Symbol

The logotype is our most important identity asset. It is our signature and distinguishes us from others. It’s our primary identification and the very manifestation of our brand.

Full Color Logo

Our logo represents the dynamism of our app—a platform designed to generate briefs for design, marketing, UX/UI, frontend, and backend projects. Like a mosaic, users can customize the scope of each project to best suit their needs. We use our logotype with our main color palette: black for the logotype, and blue, yellow, orange, and green for the rounded shapes, in that order, showing the flexibility existent in our app.

Symbol

Our icon is composed of rounded shapes layered on top of each other to form a dynamic mosaic. This design represents the flexibility and modularity of our platform, where users can seamlessly build and customize project briefs to fit their unique needs. The elements symbolize collaboration, integration, and the interconnected nature of design, marketing, UX/UI, frontend, and backend workflows.
